About Us

Welcome to Shetako Bakery, a family-owned business that specializes in creating delicious cakes, cookies, and bread. We are located in Lusaka, Zambia, and have a team of talented bakers who are passionate about creating delicious treats. At Shetako Bakery, we believe that the best ingredients make the best products. That’s why we use only the freshest ingredients in our baking, and we never use preservatives or artificial flavors. We take pride in our work and strive to provide our customers with the best possible experience. We offer a wide range of products, including cakes, cookies, and bread. Our products are made with the freshest ingredients and are baked to perfection. We offer a variety of flavors and styles to suit every taste. Thank you for choosing Shetako Bakery. We look forward to serving you soon!"
